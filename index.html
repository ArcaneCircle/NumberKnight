Welcome to Number Knight!

Your goal is to conquer towers by fighting monsters.

Click on this little blob to kill it!

Well done! When you kill a monster, you gain its power.

In order to win a fight, you must have more power than your target: if you have the same power, you'll lose!

Oh, some elementary blobs!

The last one is powerfull with its 100 of power... But don't worry: when you kill a monster, you absorb its element and your next attack will use it.

Plant monsters are weak against fire attacks: your power will count twice against this green blob!

Don't forget: the element is available only for your next attack.

If you fight en elementary monster with a weaker element, your power will be divided by two for this duel.

You start this level with fire element, which is weak against water...

There're three elements: fire, water and plant.

Each has a weakness and resistance:

fire target water target plant target
fire attack x1 /2 x2
water attack x2 x1 /2
plant attack /2 x2 x1

Hm, beware of these skeletons...
When you kill them, you don't gain their power... You lose it!

If your own power reaches 0 or below, you die!

Like blobs, skeletons can be attached to some element.

These wizards have strange magic...
When you kill them, you gain their power... but the other monsters in the same tower gain it too!

What about elementary wizards?

Oh! Potions!
You can't lose againts potions: you simply drink them and your power is modified according to their value.

Well... You still can die if your power reached 0 or below...

Potions can also contain some elements! When you drink them, their effect is multiplied or divided by two according to the same rules applied to monsters, whether the potion effect is positive or negative!

It is not that intuitive, so try to guess your next power before drinking potions on this level.
